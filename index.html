Update on Public's Reception on Sea Cross: Bible Game™

We've been receiving some wonderful feedback and reception from people during public showings of Sea Cross: Bible Game™. If you enjoy playing Sea Cross or if you have friends or kids that enjoy helping Moses dodge crabs, don't just keep it to yourself. Share with others. Especially if you have any friends who are into gaming—share it with them!
