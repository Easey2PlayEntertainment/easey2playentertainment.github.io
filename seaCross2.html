COMING SOON! Sea Cross 2™

Information about Sea Cross 2™

The classic fish and crabs from Sea Cross: Bible Game™ are returning in a new Bible-based video game that is under development: Sea Cross 2™!

The primary difference between this game and its predecessor is that instead of helping Israelites walk across a parted sea, you are helping lone fishermen on the Sea of Galilee catch fish and collect treasure. Not familiar with this story? Here's a question that might ring some bells: do you recall Jesus Christ calling a few fishermen to follow Him? These fishermen aren't any ordinary fishermen. These are fishermen who, after having several wild years out at sea, will set down their nets and enter an unforgettable journey that would ultimately kickstart the largest religious movements ever known to mankind: the Christian church.

Staying true to the 8-bit style of the retro games of the past, Sea Cross 2™ breathes new life into the genre of classic action arcade games. Players will maneuver around whales and boats, catch coins that are floating on the surface, and capture fish using their ship's net gun. And, of course, staying true to the Sea Cross series, there will be Bible questions that must be answered correctly in order to progress.

We expect Sea Cross 2™ to be completed by the end of 2026, but, due to college classes and multiple projects, the release might be pushed to a later date.

Stay tuned for more information about this release.
