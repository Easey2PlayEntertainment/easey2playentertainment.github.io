Announcements

Sea Cross: Bible Game™ Played 20 More Times at 2026 Kansas-Nebraska Conference Camp Meeting Than Previous

Once again, Sea Cross: Bible Game™ was demonstrated at the annual Kansas-Nebraska Conference Camp Meeting in Lincoln, Nebraska. Like the year prior, kids flocked around the Easey2Play Entertainment booth, wanting to get their hands upon the controls.

"The game was played around 370 times this year," notes Azariah Easey, founder and CEO of Easey2Play Entertainment. "That's 24 more times than the previous year, where it was played around 346 times."

After the continual success of the Easey2Play Entertainment booth at the camp meeting, Azariah is making plans to bring it back the following year.

"Hopefully Sea Cross: The Galilean Adventure™ will be completed by next year," Azariah muses. "Then, we could have two arcade machines: one running the first game in the Sea Cross™ series and the other one running the second. Perhaps the arcade machines can run more than one game. Perhaps both could be set up to allow players to compete against each other to see who can score the highest in either Sea Cross 1 or Sea Cross 2 [the code names for both projects]."

Stay tuned for more updates!
